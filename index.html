human factor & gap measuring

By observing the actual riding experiences of the developing car frames and competitive products to confirm the riding comfort, it is clear that both styling and usability can work well with each other and therefore make a good result. Furthermore, the length of the gap is also being measured in order to make sure the fluency and safety of using it.

package layout & 3D model confirm

Through the 3D model of the car frame the manufacturer provided, both limitations and functions of spare parts can be confirmed. Moreover, the range of vision, hip-point and accelerator heel point through the ride also be measured. By listing all the contents can make sure the later designing progress goes well.

1:1 ratio model building process

From contour drawing, color selections for spray painting, revision of modeling details, to discussion of solving problems on drawings, we cooperated with Studio X-gene, a experienced manufacturer in vehicle design, and eventually created an feasible and unprecedented all-terrain vehicle step by step.
